## **FAQs**

<details>

<summary><strong>What is Batch One-to-Multi Transfer?</strong></summary>

It aggregates multiple one-to-multi transfer tasks into a single, synchronized execution pipeline—perfect for complex capital distribution and market-making scenarios.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>What is the maximum number of pages that can be added at one time?</strong></summary>

There is no hard code limit; you can add multiple transfer pages simultaneously. The maximum capacity depends entirely on your local device performance and page complexity.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Does it support sending different amounts to different addresses?</strong></summary>

Absolutely. Each designated task page operates independently, allowing you to configure unique token types, recipient address matrices, and varying amounts.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Is It Secure?</strong></summary>

The platform uses a fully client-side signing mechanism. Your private key is never uploaded or stored on any server, and all transactions are signed locally in your browser, ensuring the platform cannot access your private key.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>How many addresses can be sent to in a single execution?</strong></summary>

Zero limits. Thanks to the private key invocation mode, the protocol completely bypasses tedious wallet-by-wallet manual confirmations, allowing unlimited recipient addresses per single execution.

</details>
