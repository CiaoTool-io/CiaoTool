# Service Fees

{% hint style="success" %}
Platform service fees do not include Gas fees or Jito tips. Gas fees vary based on the transaction priority you choose, and Jito tips can be customized according to your needs.
{% endhint %}

**Dynamic Pricing Adjustment**

Native token prices can fluctuate significantly over time. CiaoTool regularly reviews market conditions and adjusts service fees when necessary to help maintain competitive and reasonable pricing across different market environments.

**Competitive Pricing Commitment**

We are committed to providing highly cost-effective Web3 tools. If you find a lower price for a comparable service on another platform, feel free to let us know. Our team will review the pricing and make adjustments when appropriate to keep our services competitively priced.
