## What is CiaoTool Solana Batch Wallet Creator? <a href="#definition" id="definition"></a>

If you need to participate in project airdrops, perform DApp interaction testing, or wish to disperse your assets across different addresses, manually creating new addresses one by one in a wallet extension is not only extremely time-consuming but also prone to messy and confused records.

**Solana Batch Wallet Creator** is a permanently free foundational tool provided by CiaoTool. It allows you to instantly generate hundreds or even thousands of brand-new Solana wallet addresses in seconds, just like a money-printing machine. More importantly, the system automatically pairs each address with its corresponding private key, bundling them into a clean, well-organized Excel spreadsheet for you to download with a single click.

Key Use Cases:

* **Airdrop Interaction**\
  Quickly generate a massive number of clean, "white-label" wallets at zero cost, perfectly suited for claiming project airdrops or engaging in early ecosystem interactions.
* **Asset Security Isolation**\
  Allocate independent sub-wallets for different Web3 investment projects, completely preventing a total fund wipeout caused by a compromise or malicious approval on a single master wallet.
* **Multi-Account Management & Fund Routing Network Construction**\
  Provide underlying address database support for complex asset allocation, portfolio structure optimization, and market-making arrangements.

## **FAQs**

<details>

<summary><strong>Is the operation secure?</strong></summary>

CiaoTool uses a fully client-side signing mechanism. Your private key is never uploaded or stored on any server, and all transactions are signed locally in your browser. This ensures, at a technical level, that the platform has no access to your private key.

You can choose to generate wallets in a completely disconnected, air-gapped environment to ensure that the new wallet information is never exposed to public networks.
