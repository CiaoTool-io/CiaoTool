## What Is Solana Account Freeze (Blacklist)?

The **Solana Account Freeze** feature, also known as a **Blacklist**, allows token issuers to use the **Freeze Authority** to prevent specific wallets from performing token-related actions, such as transfers or trading. This feature helps mitigate malicious activities, including bot manipulation and abusive trading behavior, while providing additional control for project management and community governance.

**Common Use Cases:**

* **Bot Protection**: Block malicious wallets from manipulating markets or exploiting trading opportunities
* **Risk Management**: Freeze suspicious accounts to reduce the risk of asset misuse
* **Campaign Control**: Restrict selected wallets from participating in airdrops or incentive programs
* **Project Governance**: Blacklist rule-violating users to maintain fairness
* **Market Strategy**: Control token circulation during specific phases to support launch or pricing strategies

## Common Failure Cases

* The Freeze Authority is not held, making it impossible to execute the freeze operation.
* An incorrect address was frozen, affecting normal user operations.
* Incorrect multi-sig or authority settings, leading to an inability to unfreeze.
* The operation was not successfully confirmed on-chain, leading to a false assumption that the account was frozen.
* Frequent freezing/unfreezing has triggered on-chain restrictions or latency.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions <a href="#frequently-asked-questions" id="frequently-asked-questions"></a>

**1. What is the account freezing feature?**

* **Answer:** Freezing an account means temporarily restricting a wallet’s ability to **transfer or trade** specific tokens.

**2. Who has the authority to freeze an account?**

* **Answer:** Only accounts with the token's **Freeze authority** can perform freezing operations.

**3. Can a frozen account still receive tokens?**

* **Answer:** Yes, freezing only restricts **sending and trading**; receiving tokens is still allowed.

**4. How can I unfreeze a frozen account?**

* **Answer:** An account with **Freeze authority** can initiate the unfreeze operation at any time.

**5. Does freezing an account affect overall token circulation?**

* **Answer:** No, it only affects the **specific frozen wallet**, not the overall circulation of the token.
