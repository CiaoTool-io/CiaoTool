# Solana - Tax Fee Claim Tutorial

## What is CiaoTool Solana Token Tax Fee Claim?

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (778).png" alt="CiaoTool Solana Toolkit Page about Tax Fee Claim Feature"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**CiaoTool Solana Token Tax Fee Claim** is an on-chain asset aggregation tool custom-built for tax tokens constructed on the Token-2022 standard. It allows the project team's wallet that possesses the contract "tax withdrawal authority" to safely withdraw the tax funds accumulated in the token contract account at any time.

Under the Token-2022 standard, the taxes generated during user transactions or transfers do not enter the project team's wallet directly in real time. Instead, they are automatically deducted on-chain and temporarily locked within the token's own account or related storage structures. By invoking Solana's official underlying tax withdrawal instructions, this tool enables project teams to securely and completely withdraw these accumulated token taxes from network-wide transfers into the token account with a single click, achieving centralized management of project operational funds.
