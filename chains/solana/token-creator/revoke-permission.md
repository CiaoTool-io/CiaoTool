## What is Revoke Authorities on Solana?

In Solana tokens, "Revoke authorities" refers to actively destroying or transferring key control over a token, including the update authority (metadata), freeze authority, and mint authority. Once renounced, these privileges are permanently unrecoverable, making the token more decentralized and frequently utilized to enhance market trust.

Common Uses:

* **Boosting Trust:** Renouncing the mint authority to eliminate doubts regarding "infinite minting."
* **Locking Information:** Renouncing the update authority to ensure token metadata is immutable.
* **Memecoin Project Endorsement:** Serving as a crucial signal for a "fair launch."
* **Pre-launch Operations:** Enhancing credibility during the pool-opening or marketing phases.
