## FAQs

<details>

<summary><strong>What do I receive after removing V3 liquidity?</strong></summary>

You may receive one or both assets from the pool. The asset composition depends on the pool price relative to the position’s configured price range at execution time.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Can I remove only part of a V3 position?</strong></summary>

Yes. You can reduce a selected percentage of the position’s liquidity while leaving the remaining liquidity under the same price range and fee tier.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Can I change the price range when removing liquidity?</strong></summary>

No. Removing liquidity only decreases the liquidity of the current position.

To use a different price range, remove the desired liquidity and create a new V3 position with the new range.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Can I change the fee tier?</strong></summary>

No. The fee tier is permanently associated with the selected V3 pool and position.

To use a different fee tier, create a position in another V3 pool that uses the required fee tier.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Does removing V3 liquidity change the pool price?</strong></summary>

Removing liquidity does not normally perform a token swap, so it does not intentionally move the pool price.

However, it reduces the liquidity available within the position’s price range. This may increase the price impact of subsequent trades, especially if a large share of active liquidity is removed.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Can another wallet remove my V3 liquidity?</strong></summary>

Only the position owner or an authorized address or contract can manage the position NFT.

Never transfer or authorize your position NFT to an unknown address or contract.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>How does CiaoTool protect the imported private key?</strong></summary>

The private key is processed locally in the browser and is not uploaded, transmitted, stored, logged, or written to Local Storage. Imported private-key data is cleared when the page is closed or refreshed.

</details>
